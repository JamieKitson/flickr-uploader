Backup your Android photos



Do you have a safe copy of your photos and videos in case your phone breaks or get stolen?

Flickr Uploader is a small app that automatically uploads a backup of every photo or video you take. And with Flickr's astounding free terabyte of storage, you are set for at least 10 years!
