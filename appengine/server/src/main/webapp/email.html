Hello Flickr & Pictarine friend,

You probably take a lot of pictures with your Android device. But what if you loose your phone or what if it breaks? Do you have a safe copy of your valuable memories?

Flickr Uploader is a small app that automatically uploads a full-resolution backup of every photo or video you take. And with Flickr's astounding free terabyte of storage, you are set for at least ten years!
